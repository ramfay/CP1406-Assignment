Sign Up for CossiePlay Parties!

At CossiePlay, we love to party - and what better way to show off your new cosplay than to come to one of our CossiePlay parties! Head on down to the Town Hall this 2nd of April, from 6pm - 11pm, for our next party.

What's in it for me?

If you come along to our party, you will be treated to a LIVE DJ (who takes requests), entitlement for one non-alcoholic drink, and a glow stick. You'll also get to meet new people, make new friends, and even dance. The dancing part is up to you.
  • This month you can purchase 3 drinks for the price of 2 at the bar!
  • First-time party-goers get a 50% discount on entry
  • If you get a costume custom made, your next party entry is free if you wear that costume.

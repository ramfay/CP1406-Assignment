So what exactly IS Cosplay?

Cosplay = Costume + Play

Cosplayers are the mighty fine people who don marvellous costumes and intricate accessories to represent a character. There are a multitude of conventions dedicated to this friendly subculture, where characters are really only limited by the Cosplayer's imagination.

Cosplay Ideas:

  • Traditional character portrayal
  • Gender-bend of a character
  • An interpretation of something abstract

"Characters? What characters?"

Any of your favourite characters, of course! Take inspiration from your favourite movie, TV show, a great book you've read - comics included - or even anime and manga!

Cosplaying began as a hobby in 1990, and has rapidly grown since then. So why not join this great community and get your first custom cosplay from us.
